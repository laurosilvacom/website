## The Daily System

**Every morning, before anything else:**

1. **Send 1 outreach message.** Just one. That's the minimum. If I do more, great. But one is non-negotiable.
2. **Check one number:** How much have I earned/invoiced this month?
3. **No spending over $30 without a 24-hour wait.**

That's it. Three things. Every day.

When avoidance shows up — and it will — make the action smaller.
Can't send 5 messages? Send 1.
Can't write a full message? Open the laptop and type the name.
Start moving. The rest follows.
