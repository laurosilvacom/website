If Lauro feels the urge to overspend or avoid looking at finances, the AI can remind him to use this script:

1. Stop.
2. Breathe once.
3. Ask:
   - `What am I feeling right now?`
   - `What problem am I trying to solve with this purchase?`

   - `Will this make the next 30 days easier or harder?`

`Pause. Name the feeling. Wait. Then decide.`
